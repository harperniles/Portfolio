About Me

I’m an undergraduate student at UC San Diego majoring in Education Sciences and minoring in Spanish Literature. Outside of school, I work as a climbing coach and a crew member at Trader Joe’s. I am extremely passionate about education and have hope to be and educator. My adaptability and creativity will help me in this field. And my understanding of restorative and inclusive practices will help me make a difference. I love combining teaching, communication and creativity when designing a lesson, planning a climbing game, or exploring new skills in my programming and design classes.

This site brings together some of the work I’ve done so far: from HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap projects to visual design, Excel assignments, and more.
